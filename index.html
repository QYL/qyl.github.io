How It Works

A seamless flow from daily moments to yearly insights.

Daily Flow

Each day, receive one thoughtful prompt from the 40-question framework. Spend 2-5 minutes capturing your thoughts through text, voice, photos, or videos. The app intelligently tags entries for later retrieval.

Weekly Flow

Every week, engage with 4-6 targeted reflection questions. Our AI analyzes your daily logs to pre-fill drafts, making reflection effortless. Track your life scores across all 8 pillars.

Yearly Flow

At year's end, all your reflections compile into a beautiful, interactive e-book. All 40 questions are answered with your authentic voice, enriched with photos and memories‚Äîa true capsule of your year.
