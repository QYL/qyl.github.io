Transform Your Year, One Day at a Time

Selflog is a transformative iOS app that merges the power of a 40-question year-in-review framework with daily intentional living, memory preservation, and personal growth. Rather than treating reflection as a December ritual, Selflog weaves the 40 questions into daily life through 8 Life Pillars.

Every aspect of your life, organized into meaningful categories for holistic growth

Adventure & Exploration

How It Works

A seamless flow from daily moments to yearly insights

Daily Flow

Each day, receive one thoughtful prompt from the 40-question framework. Spend 2-5 minutes capturing your thoughts through text, voice, photos, or videos. The app intelligently organizes your entries across the 8 Life Pillars, building a rich tapestry of your daily experiences.

↓

Weekly Flow

Every week, engage with 4-6 targeted reflection questions. Our AI analyzes your daily logs to pre-fill drafts, making reflection effortless. Track your life scores across all pillars and spot trends in your personal growth journey.

↓

Yearly Flow

At year's end, all your reflections compile into a beautiful, interactive e-book. All 40 questions are answered with your authentic voice, enriched with photos and memories. Set unlock dates for future reflection, share with a partner, or export for safekeeping.
